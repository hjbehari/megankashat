Meet the artist

A world of sound, movement, and color.

Megan Kashat is an Arab American artist based in Detroit whose creative language moves across painting, music, dance, poetry, and storytelling.

Her work carries a sense of motion — layered, emotional, textured, and alive with the energy of performance.
